Minnesota Countertop Installation

Custom countertops for Minneapolis, St. Paul, Princeton, and the Twin Cities.

Clear Choice Countertops is a Minnesota countertop installer specializing in laminate countertops, solid surface countertops, Corian countertops, Wilsonart Thinscape, butcher block countertops, cultured marble, and other non-stone countertop materials.

Homeowners choose Clear Choice when they want a clean stone-look finish, clear pricing, in-house fabrication, and local installation without the cost of granite, marble, or quartz. The team serves kitchens, bathroom vanities, islands, bars, rental properties, commercial counters, and remodel projects throughout the greater Twin Cities metro.

What countertop materials does Clear Choice install?

Clear Choice installs laminate, solid surface, Corian, Wilsonart Thinscape, butcher block, wood, cultured marble, and other non-stone countertop surfaces.

Where does Clear Choice Countertops work?

The team serves Princeton, Minneapolis, St. Paul, Anoka, Blaine, Maple Grove, Stillwater, Woodbury, and communities across the greater Twin Cities metro.

How fast can a countertop be installed?

Most countertop installations take place 4 to 7 business days after measurement, depending on project size, selected material, and schedule availability.

Are laminate and solid surface countertops good alternatives to stone?

Yes. Modern laminate and solid surface countertops can create marble, granite, quartz, or concrete looks at a lower price, with practical maintenance for everyday homes.
